Benjamin is a PhD Candidate in Management Studies at Mines Paris and Paris Dauphine - PSL University. His research focus on the exploration of sufficiency from the perspective of businesses and consumption behaviours.

Benjamin holds an MSc in Environmental Management and Engineering, an MSc in Management and Marketing, and a BA in History, Archaeology and Cultural Project Management. He has worked on conservation and sustainability-related projects in India, Thailand, France and the United Kingdom for UNESCO, the University of Cambridge, TotalEnergies, and a number of NGOs.
