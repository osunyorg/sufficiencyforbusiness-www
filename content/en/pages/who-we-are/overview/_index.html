Our purpose

We are an international network of researchers dedicated to advancing sufficiency in business. Our aim is to generate innovative ideas, conduct impactful research, and exchange evidence-based knowledge to challenge dominant business paradigms.  Our purpose is to reimagine and transform business through sufficiency.

Milestones

April 2021
Creation of the network by Laura Niessen and Laura Beyeler

July 2021
First online meeting of the network

July 2023
Launch of the network mailing list

July 2025
Formalisation of the network through a shared purpose

September 2025
Launch of the website and LinkedIn page
