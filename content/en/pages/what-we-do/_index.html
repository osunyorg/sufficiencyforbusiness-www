Research and educational resources

We work collectively on curating and generating new material to disseminate sufficiency principles in education and research. Check our current projects →

Monthly network meetings

Members of the Sufficiency in Business Network meet on average once a month to present ongoing or recent research, launch joint projects, and share updates. View our upcoming meetings →

Conference tracks and workshops

As a collective, we regularly organise special tracks, workshops and events, both online and in hybrid. These seek to explore the implications of sufficiency in business, both from an academic and a practitioner's perspective. Check our upcoming events →

Mailing list

Our open mailing list allows network members to share calls for papers, communications, projects, tenders, funding opportunities, and related opportunities to the benefit of the community. Register here for our mailing list
