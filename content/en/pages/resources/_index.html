Books and special issues

The Network has undertaken several book projects on the topic of sufficiency in business.

book icon svg

In the meantime, check out Sufficiency in Business - The Transformative Potential of Business for Sustainability , edited by network members Maike Grossen and Laura Niessen
https://www.transcript-publishing.com/978-3-8376-6910-7/sufficiency-in-business/

Teaching material

We are working on a deck slide to equip educators interested in teaching on the topic of sufficiency in business.  Get in touch  if you're interested in joining or hearing more about the project

Research database

An open-access research database is under construction to share seminal and pioneering research articles and references on the topic of sufficiency in business

Fellow networks

ENOUGH is the main dedicated network gathering academics and experts working on sufficiency, from a variety of disciplines
https://enough.owlstown.net/


World Sufficiency Lab
The World Sufficiency Lab is a non-profit and independent think-to-do tank, whose mission is to serve as the primary resource and voice for sufficiency worldwide
https://www.thesufficiencylab.org/
